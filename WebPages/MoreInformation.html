My Plate Recommended Daily Intake & Diet

  • According to the U.S. Department of Agriculture's dietary guidelines for Americans, half of your "plate" or meal should consist of fruits and vegetables. The focus should be on fresh fruit and vegetables rather than their canned options. Choose a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables to ensure a well-rounded diet. U.S. Department of Agriculture. (2016, January). Dietary Guidelines. ChooseMyPlate. https://www.choosemyplate.gov/eathealthy/dietary-guidelines
  • Smoothies are an easy way to get daily nutrition in an easy and convienent way. Smoothies may consist of recommended daily intake of fruits, vegetables, protein, and dairy all in one drink.
